I am Darrick He

I am currently an undergraduate student studying Computer Science, as well as a musician, and cook. Since childhood, the idea of a polymath or 'Renaissance Man' has always intrigued me. Leonardo Da Vinci being the first of the kind that I had heard of. Although seemingly unusual amongst my peers, I never had singular focus on one particular discipline. I would rather be good at 3 different things, than be the best at 1 thing. Thus, I made myself to be both an enthusiast and craftsman for the three things in my life that I am passionate about: technology, music, and food. Am I being unrealistic? Maybe, but it's a risk I'm willing to take.

If I'm not cycling or cooking, the majority of my time is spent in front of a computer screen.
